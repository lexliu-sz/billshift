# BillShift

Freelance consultants who use LLMs ship work faster — billing the same hourly rate quietly undervalues their skills. Enter your current rate and BillShift shows the value-adjusted equivalent based on your LLM productivity multiplier. A quick sanity check before quoting your next project.

iPhone & iPad. Free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no network.
